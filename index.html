Where can NexGen Vending Machines be placed?

NexGen Vending Machines can be placed in various locations such as offices, schools, hospitals, shopping malls, and public transportation hubs. They are designed to fit in both indoor and outdoor environments.

What payment methods do your machines support?

Our machines support bill, coin, cashless payments, and UPI. Some models also offer optional face-scan pay, AI QR pay, and membership/IC card payment functions.

Do the vending machines offer refrigeration?

Yes, most of our vending machines feature refrigeration with temperatures typically ranging from 4°C to 25°C, and some frozen food machines can reach as low as -18°C.
